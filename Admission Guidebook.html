Bachelor's level or Undergraduate is one of the most important phases in academic field. Bachelor’s level study begins soon after the successful completion of intermediate level. Every year, all the educational boards announce the intermediate annual exams result in September and after the declaration of inter results the procedure of admissions in bachelor’s programmes begins. Bachelors level Courses/Groups The private and government sector institutes in Pakistan offer wide range of two, three and four years courses at bachelor's level from which the aspirants chooses the courses according to their field of interest. Some major fields in which the students can get admissions at bachelor's level includes:

  • Medical
  • Engineering
  • Computer Sciences & IT
  • Social Sciences
  • Law
  • Agricultural Studies
  • Media Studies
  • Finance and Accounting
  • Arts & Designing
  • Architecture
